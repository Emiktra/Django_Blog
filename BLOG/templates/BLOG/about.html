About our development team

We, The Djangalactic Team, is a passionate team focused on developing online social platforms to let people tell their story, or maybe share your knowledge with the world.

About Djangalactic Blog

Djangalactic Blog is a Social Blog Platform created for you to share your blogs which can include many topic such as comedy, fiction, educational or maybe just a blog about yourself. Feel free to share what you want and learn new people.

How to use our Blog

When you first come to our page it might feel different from other blogs you are familiar with, so we composed a proper guide as how to navigate in our blog and write Blogs effortlessly.

To Login:
  • If you are logged out, click Login on the Navigation Bar at the top of the Website. This will direct you to the login page
  • From there fill out the form and make sure to write your username case sensitively and press Log in to log in
  • If the login process fails, you will either be notified or nothing will happen. If you entered the credentials correctly you will login and will be redirected to the home page
  • If you forgot your password, you can click to Forgot Password to start the password reset process. Keep in mind that you need to have an email connected to your account to receive instructions. If you don't have an email connected to your account, contact us at DjangalacticTeam@gmail.com and ask for an account recovery.
To Register:
  • If you want to create a new account click Register on the navigation Bar at the top of the Website. This will send you to the Register page
  • From there fill the form and click Register to check oyur form. This will create an account and automatically log you in
  • While filling the form, make sure to:
    • enter your email if you ever need to recover your account
    • meet all the requirements stated under the password field
    • pick a unique profile picture if you want to be recognized
  • If the register process fails, you will either be notified or nothing will happen. If you entered the credentials correctly you will create an account andlogin and will be redirected to the home page
To Log Out:
  • If you ever want to log out from your currently logged in account for any reason, click Logout on the Navigation Bar at the top of the Website. This will log you out and will redirect you to the home page
To go back to Home Page:
  • To return back to the home page, you can click Home on the Navigation Bar at the top of the Website
To see more details about a post:
  • To see more details about a post simply click on the post and you will be directed to a page where you can see more details about the post, read comments and give the post a like
  • If you are not logged in, you can't comment or like the post
  • If you are the owner of the post, you can use the details page to edit or delete your post
To comment under a post:
  • To like a comment under a post navigate to the post you want want to comment under and click on it
  • There you can find a field for commenting. Write a comment and click Post to comment
  • If this field doesn't exist, it means you aren't logged in and thus cannot comment a post. To post a comment, create an account

    • Tip: You can't delete a comment once you post it

To like a post:
  • To like a post, navigate to the post and click on it. This will bring you to the detailed blog page.
  • If you are logged in, there will be a button to like the post
  • To un-like the post, click the like button again. You should see the like count go down
To see your profile:
  • To see your profile click Profile to navigate to the Profile page. You have to be logged in to enter this page.
  • In the page you can see personal info as well as private info about your account. To edit these information click on Edit Profile button at the bottom of the profile page. There you can alter or add info in account. To save click Update Profile
  • If you enter illegal info, you will either get an error or nothing will happen. If you enter correct info you will be directed to the profile page
To Post a Blog:
  • To post a new blog click New Post on the Navigation Bar at the top of the Website. You will be redirected to a form
  • From there fill out your Blog form with the appropriate info and click Post button at the bottom of the page
  • You can choose your Post to be Published, which will let make it public. Or you can make it a Draft and only you will be able to see your drafts on the home page. You can later Edit them and make them public
  • You don't have to add an image but it is highly recommended that you do.
